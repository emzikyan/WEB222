Soccer

Soccer, which is also referred to as football, is a team sport that is played between two teams of eleven players each. To win the game, you must score more goals than your opponents by putting the ball in their net. The ball is moved largely by kicking it with the feet, however other body parts, including as the head and torso, can also be utilized. The game is played on a rectangular field with goals at either end. Millions of people play and watch soccer on a regular basis, making it one of the most popular sports in the world. It is also renowned for its demanding physical requirements, dexterous footwork, and fervent fan culture.
